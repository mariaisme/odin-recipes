Fried Egg


Description

An egg fried in a pan with oil or fat

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Seasonings to taste

Steps

  1. Put oil in clean pan and turn stove to medium heat.
  2. Once pan is hot enough, crack egg open on pan.
  3. Cook until how you like your egg.
  4. Put seasonings such as salt and pepper to your liking.
  5. Turn off the stove and plate your fried egg.

Notes

  • If you want your egg more toasty, add more oil.
    Once you crack your egg, use a spoon to spoon the hot oil on top of the egg
  • You can use as much eggs as you want.
    But do consider talking to your doctor or physician about how much you can eat per day.
  • For seasonings, you can go for the classic salt and pepper.
    But don't let that limit your tastes.
    Consider trying other spices such as chili powder or a dash of nori flakes.
